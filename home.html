What are Search Engines

Search engines work in three ways, these ways are by crawling which is to search the internet for code and content. Then by Indexing is to store and organize and displays the data that was obtained by first crawling. The finally the search engines process is by ranking the data that they obtained in the order of relevance.

Importance of Keywords/ Meta Tags Description

Keywords and Metataags are important because they act like a filter for the abundant amount of information that is located on the internet. Also, they help the user and search engine to filter down to the specific information that they are looking for. A search engine saves a user time by only pulling and filtering thousands of websites with high quality of websites that a user is looking for.

Good Heading Hierarchy

Headings give both visitors and search engines important clues about the content and relevancy. There are six "importance levels", from the most important H1 heading to the least important H6 heading. It is important to only have one H1 heading so that search engines can focus on that heading when performing searches.
